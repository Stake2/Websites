Chapter: 1 - The SpaceShips








Hello, I am Izaque, Stake2, and Funkysnipa Cat.
This is SpaceLiving, a story of mine, I created it on 19/01/2019 with a friend of mine, Julia.
She is or was a spirit that existed in my house, I talked with her through psychography, a way to listen to the spirit's voices in your head.
And she gave me the idea for the story, she talked about Lisa, her living inside somewhere in space, in her room.
I perfected the story, made it better, and created the idea of the spaceship, other people living inside spaceships.
And also about the spaceship network, the systems of the spaceship, just like I do with my theory, New World.
I also create systems and technologies for the New World theory, which is the theory about a better world than this one.
SpaceLiving also takes inspiration from the video Shelter made by Porter Robinson, Madeon, A-1 Pictures, and Crunchyroll.
Porter Robinson & Madeon - Shelter (Official Video) (Short Film with A-1 Pictures & Crunchyroll)
Taken from the video:
"Shelter tells the story of Rin, a 17-year-old girl who lives her life inside of a futuristic simulation completely by herself in infinite, beautiful loneliness.
Each day, Rin awakens in virtual reality and uses a tablet that controls the simulation to create a new, different, beautiful world for herself.
Until one day, everything changes, and Rin comes to learn the true origins behind her life inside a simulation."
Lisa is a little like that, a girl trapped inside a spaceship, where she can not leave and has everything she needs, food, entertainment, and other things.
This thing about her not being able to leave the spaceship changed a little after some chapters.
She can also modify her surroundings, the spaceship is modular, and can create things that Lisa wants.
I have been writing this story for two years already, and I also revised all of its chapters and perfected it a lot.
And I am ready to share this story with you, I love it a lot, and I also wanted to live inside a LonelyShip.
I hope you enjoy it as much as I enjoyed writing it.

In the year 2019, there was a tale of some SpaceShips that had an entire house inside them, those spaceships were called the LonelyShips.
Some rumors about those spaceships were spreading across the Internet, people were talking about them on the Internet forums and making theories.
Those spaceships had a super fast and powerful computer on each one of them, it was white, a PlayStation 4 gaming console, a bed, and a wardrobe.
They had white walls inside and out, the computers had a modified version of Windows 10 installed on them.
It was called Windows 10 (SpaceLiving Edition).


The bed had a blue blanket, the bed was made of some resistant plastic that was light gray, and the wardrobe was gray too.
The spaceships also had a Laser Turret outside of them, it could shoot a laser beam to hit approaching asteroids.
The Laser Turret had two modes, the Expanded Range mode, and the Shielded mode.
The Expanded Range mode made it possible for the Laser Turret to shoot asteroids that were in a place that was hard to aim.
The Shielded mode made it possible for the Laser Turret to cover itself with a shield to protect it from asteroids.
Two tall windows to see the sun, the moon, and the Earth, an ergonomic chair with casters, adjustable armrests, height, and back.
The spaceship had also a bathroom, and sixteen turbines to move the spaceship when needed, they were deactivated by default.
The LonelyShip could compress the bathroom to a small place.
Making it possible to the resident of the LonelyShip to open the door of the bathroom and enter inside it, without making the spaceship too large.
The bathroom existed in a type of space that did not occupy physical space, it was the width of the door.
Also, the spaceship had a teleporter that could teleport the resident of the spaceship between other spaceships and also to the Earth.
The teleporter was called LonelyShip Teleporter, the initials were LST and were written on the upper part of its plastic frame, the LST was blue.
That teleporter did not exist in older versions of the LonelyShip.
The wardrobe had four doors and was on the right of the LST teleporter.
There was also a fridge that could create anything you thought of, it was called the Thought-Powered Fridge or TPF, the TPF was blue.
It was on the left of the computer chair, and at the right of the left window.
The fridge made foods and drinks that were only nutrients, for example, a slice of pizza.
That slice of pizza would have the smell, taste, and texture of a pizza, but would be just nutrients and vitamins that the resident needed.
The TPF fridge would analyze the body of the resident and make a diet containing the nutrients and vitamins that the resident needs.
Then it would split those nutrients that were needed for the whole day into three meals, breakfast, lunch, and dinner.
If the resident wanted to eat more than three meals, the fourth and next meals would be only mass, not nutrients.
Foods created by the TPF fridge did not have meat or animal products, they were vegan-friendly.
Particles on the food could change the nutrients that are in it, but could not change the taste, smell, or texture, that was pre-built into the food.
The spaceship or LonelyShip had an Artificial Intelligence, an AI, that could help the residents of the spaceships manage their spaceships.
The AI was called LonelyShip Artificial Intelligence, or LSAI for short.
The LonelyShip also had four small signboards on its walls, two displaying "SpaceLiving", and another two displaying "LonelyShip" on the right of the SpaceLiving one.
The SpaceLiving and LonelyShip signboards were equal on each side of the LonelyShip, one SpaceLiving one on each side, and one LonelyShip one on each side.
The signboards on the left side were on the left of the window, and the right ones were on the right of the window.


Also, the LonelyShip had two big signboards on the front side of it, one with "SpaceLiving" written on it, and another one with "LonelyShip".
The SpaceLiving signboard is above the LonelyShip one.

That spaceship, the LonelyShip, was part of a network of SpaceShips, the network was called the SpaceLiving SpaceShip Network.
A lot of other people lived inside those spaceships, the spaceships were not close enough for you to see one outside your window.
The company that created those spaceships and the SpaceLiving SpaceShip Network was called Think Outside The Box or TOTB for short.
It created that spaceship network for teens that were depressed and sad about their lives, they did not have a will or a purpose to live for.
The company teleported the depressed teens to the LonelyShips by teleporting them while they were sleeping.
They were teleported to their LonelyShip, and they thought that it was a dream, then, the LSAI told them that they were not in a dream.
After that, the teens asked if they could go back to Earth, the LSAI told them that they could go back.
The computers of the LonelyShips also had the SpaceLiving Network app or program installed by default, for those who did not like to manage their LonelyShips with an AI.
The residents of the SpaceLiving Network were called LonelyShippers or LonelySpaceShippers.
They also asked what they could do there, and the LSAI explained what they could do there.
If you live in a LonelyShip, you can ask the LSAI for anything, like changing wall colors, removing or adding furniture.
The Things Generator, a spectral machine or system that exists inside the LonelyShip, will create those things for you.
It can also delete things that you do not want with its powerful laser beam.
Some of the teens decided to stay there, and live a better life in the SpaceLiving Network, they thought they would have fun there.
Using the computer, playing games, listening to music, watching anime, cartoons, series, movies, and videos.
Some of them thought that it was bad to have to eat food, drink water, and sleep, they still needed to do that, but now they could do it easier.
The TOTB company thought that they would live a better life on their spaceships.

One of those people is Lisa, a sixteen years old girl, she is a Brazilian girl, she had medium gray hair and green eyes.
She played games on her computer, used the TPF fridge to eat, she also liked to write stories on her computer, Lisa posted them on the Wattpad website for the world to see.
(Wattpad is a website for people to write and read stories, for authors to post their stories and gain readers.)
The girl lived her life there, doing the stuff that she loved to do, the things that made her happy, and the things that had meaning to her.
Lisa had a beautiful LonelyShip with blue walls, it had a super-fast computer with two screens, a PlayStation 4 video game console, and a fridge called Thought-Powered Fridge, or TPF.
The spaceship also had a bathroom with a laser toilet bowl that melts the things inside it, a double-size bed, a wardrobe, and a portable video game console called Nintendo 3DS.
Lisa had the normal things that every LonelyShip has, she asked for the LSAI to change her wall colors, she asked for the blue color.
The walls of the LonelyShip were made of a special material that could change its color while in contact with some specific substances.
She has asked the LSAI to change its name, the default name of the LSAI is George and its default gender is male.
Some people theorize on the Internet that George was the father of the creator of TOTB that had passed away.
So they named the LSAI by their father's name.
Lisa gave the name Ana to the LSAI, it was the name of her mother, to remind Lisa of her.
The girl thought about her father, he was named John, she loved her father, but thought he did not like her.
She liked to use her computer because it was super fast and had lots of hard drive space for her to download and store her series, anime, cartoons, and movies.
Lisa loved to live there, write stories, play games, and listen to some Dubstep and Japanese songs on her computer.
Today she had nothing important to do, but she wanted to play some games on the Steam gaming platform.
(Steam is software where you can download free and paid games to play on your computer.)
Lisa turned her computer on and sat on the chair, clicked on the Steam program, and waited for it to open.
The chair that she had was very comfortable, it was like a gaming chair, it had adjustable things and a backrest lever to tilt back and relax.
She opened a game called Titanfall 2 to play a little, she loved that game, it had lots of futuristic things and mechas, robots, and laser weapons.
Lisa entered a multiplayer match and made a 10/3 score, 10 are the kills and 3 are the deaths.
She liked that match, then she closed Steam and opened the programs Mozilla Firefox and Spotify.
(Mozilla Firefox is a web browser, Spotify is a music player where you can listen to online music without downloading it.)
Lisa selected a Dubstep song on Firefox to blast on her speakers and started banging her head to the beat of the song.
She liked the fact that she could hear music at full volume and no one would hear and be annoyed.
(Because in space, no one can hear you scream, just kidding, hehe. ^^)

Then she opened a social network website called Facebook to talk with her friends and started to talk with one friend of hers called Anny.
(Facebook is a social network where you can create an account and make a profile with information about you, it can be used to talk and make friends online.)
"What's up?" asked Lisa.
"I'm fine, how about you?" asked Anny.
"Me too, what are you up to?" asked Lisa.
"Just playing some God of War on my PS4, what about you, Lisa?" asked Anny with a smile emoji.
"Oh, that's cool, I'm just listening to some Dubstep," replied Lisa.
"That sounds great, what song are you listening to?" asked Anny.
"The song is called 'Still Got Something'," replied Lisa.
"I'll listen to it after I finish playing, can you send it to me?" asked Anny.
Then Lisa looked at the Firefox tab that had the song playing on the YouTube website.
She clicked on the text box on her conversation with Anny and typed:
"Yes, I'll send the link to you Anny."
"Okay, thanks ^^," said Anny.
Lisa then sent the YouTube link of the Dubstep song to Anny.
"I'm gonna do something else now, good game for you, bye ^^," said the gray-haired girl.
"Bye Lisa ^^, thanks for the link, hehe," replied Anny.
"No problem, hehe," said Lisa.

Lisa closed the Mozilla Firefox program, turned her PlayStation 4 on, and grabbed its controller, the PS4 was being displayed on a second screen that Lisa had.
She put the PS4 screen on her center screen, she only used her right screen to put something she was not going to look at often, doing the opposite would hurt her neck.
The girl also opened her music player on her computer which was called Foobar2000 and closed the Spotify music player program.
She had lots of gigabytes of music stored in her solid-state drive, Lisa liked to have such a big library of music.
The girl had five solid-state drives (SSDs) on her computer, she could see each one of them using the file explorer of her computer.
Each one of them had 10 TB (Terabytes) of hard drive space, she had one for music, one for games, one for the modified version of Windows 10, one for her files, and one for media, movies, cartoons, anime, series.
Then Lisa opened the game called Grand Theft Auto V on the PS4 and started playing, she was playing the online mode of that game.
Lisa thought about when she played The Grand Theft Auto V game on her computer, it ran clean and fast, without lagging.
She liked to have such a powerful computer with a good graphics card, a fast SSD, and a fast CPU, the AMD Ryzen‚Ñ¢ Threadripper‚Ñ¢ with sixty-four cores and one hundred twenty-eight threads.
The CPU (Central Processing Unit) worked with a base clock at 2.9GHz, but Lisa's was overclocked, it worked at a 4.3GHz clock.
The girl was playing with her friends, and she was listening to the Dubstep song called "Geoxor - Turn Around" on the Foobar2000 music player.
She liked that song and sang a little bit of it while listening to the sound of the Dubstep basses, she was happy playing and listening to music.
The girl felt that she was a little hungry, she became AFK (Away From Keyboard) in the game and laid her controller on the desk.
She got up and opened her TPF fridge, it was an automatic fridge that created any food that the person thought of, it had the Food Generator spectral machine inside it.
You could think of a pizza and a pizza would appear instantly inside the fridge.
Then Lisa grabbed the cup of chocolate milk and the hamburger that were inside the fridge, she sat at her desk and started eating the hamburger and drinking the milk.
After eating, she registered the time that she ate with a Python script called "Set Food Time.py", she continued to play the game.
She played for some minutes, then Lisa told her friends that she was going to do something else.
The girl closed the Grand Theft Auto V game and turned her PS4 off.
She put to play a live stream of songs of the Future Bass genre on the Twitch website and rested a little while laying in her bed.
The girl had over-ear wireless headphones, they were good for sound isolation, they had a good sound stage and acoustics.
Lisa did not need the sound isolation or noise-canceling feature as there were no sounds in her LonelyShip, only hers.
(Twitch is a website where you can watch live streams, the majority of them are of gaming, but there is also chat, instruments, song live streams, trips, react.)

Lisa also liked to watch movies, series, anime, cartoons, and YouTube videos.
She liked to download them with a program called Utorrent and another one called MegaSync.
The girl liked to download HD, Full HD, and 60 FPS videos from YouTube with a program called 4K Video Downloader.
After resting a little, Lisa thought about watching a movie, she got up and closed the live stream of the Future Bass music genre.
She liked Marvel and its movies, she had downloaded a movie called Avengers: Infinity War from Marvel on her computer, she stored it on her media SSD.
Lisa grabbed a purple plastic bowl filled with Doritos and a bottle of Coca-cola from inside the TPF fridge to eat and drink while watching the movie.
Then the girl opened her video player that was called VLC Media Player and opened the video file of Avengers: Infinity War with it.
The gray-haired girl watched the movie while eating, making some pauses to go to the bathroom.
Lisa commented on some parts of the movie, she typed the time of the movie, like 01:15:35, and typed what she thought, or how she felt.
She even opened the Discord program on her computer to talk about the movie with her friends.
(Discord is a social network platform for gamers but non-gamers also use it.)
The girl loved the movie, all the action, the heroes, the special effects, she was sad about Spider-Man.
Lisa created a text file on her computer to write everything that she thought about the movie, her feelings, and her opinions.
Then she saved the file and closed it, after that, she opened an episode of the series called The Walking Dead to watch.
The girl watched the entire episode while eating the rest of her Doritos and drinking the rest of her Coca-cola bottle.
Lisa liked that series, she watched it when she was younger, it brings back good memories to her, even if it is a little violent and scary.
It reminds her of a special someone, of the time she watched that series with her older brother when Lisa lived on Earth.
She did not clearly remember how he was, how he looked like, or what his name was.
It has been some time since she saw him, since she had bad grades and thought her life was bad.
It was at that moment when Lisa got teleported to her LonelyShip.
After she started living on her LonelyShip, she started forgetting about her family, her brother.

Then she opened another live stream of the Future Bass music genre on YouTube.
After some time drinking water and listening to Future Bass music, she got tired and said good night to her friends on Discord.
Then she turned both her computer and her PS4 off, putting the controller inside a drawer of the computer table.
She said good night to Ana, the LSAI.
Ana turned the LonelyShip lights for Lisa to sleep, she could only look through the windows to see some light.
The LSAI, Ana, turned the turbines of the spaceship on and left the LonelyShip spinning slowly, so Lisa could see the Earth, moon, and sun sometimes through the windows.
Her body was tired, Lisa laid in her bed and closed her eyes, she had a dream where she was going to school, had to clean her room, had to get high grades.
For her that dream was a nightmare, she did not like that style of life and did not like to do the hard stuff.
Then, after a while, that dream disappeared and another dream took its place, a better dream.
Lisa was dreaming of using her supercomputer, listening to Dubstep songs, and talking to her friends on Discord.
She liked that dream, she was happy and wanted to wake up just to do the same thing that she was doing in the dream.
Lisa loved the life she lived, she had a spaceship that had a bed, a wardrobe, the TPF fridge, a supercomputer, a PlayStation 4, Nintendo 3DS, and a laser gun.
The girl had a lot of things to make her happy and entertain her, to make her feel complete, to make her life feel worth.
Lisa wanted to play games, watch, write, listen, draw, and talk, create, explore the world of the Internet, experience things, to feel things.
She wanted to live alone inside her spaceship, inside her LonelyShip, she wanted to do a lot of fun things, to eat delicious food and drink delicious drinks.
And this is the story of her life, it is also the story of the SpaceLiving SpaceShip Network, the network of SpaceShips that make you happy, SpaceLiving means living in space.
Those SpaceShips are called LonelyShips by TOTB because they are lonely, there is only you inside of it, you and the things you like.
See you in the next chapter, thank you for reading! :3
