Two adventurer siblings were walking in the forest as they always do, a little far from their house, they are a girl and a boy.
The boy is called Izaque Bulkan, the girl is called Ana Bulkan, Izaque haves brown hair, Ana has blond hair.
Izaque is the older brother, Ana is the younger one, they are living in the year 1571, on day fifteen of February.
These two kids live in the universe of Gladius Fixous, Euclideos Galaxy.
The two siblings live on the planet Earth, in the country of Gemastria, state of Yolkland, close to Folkloria city.
They see a mysterious hole on the ground, they discover that it is a hole that leads to a stairwell, and there is a big stone on top of the entrance.

The boy decides to remove the stone that was on top of the hole, after that, they go inside it.
Ana and Izaque continue to walk inside the dungeon, it is a little dark.
Izaque grabs a lit torch from the wall to illuminate the way.
The two walk a little more until they find a wall that looks like it was reconstructed, there are a lot of skulls and skeletons around it.
"Maybe there's something behind this wall, it looks soft," says Izaque.
"Yeah, let's walk more to see if we can find something to break it, bro," says Ana.
"Okay little sis, hehe," says Izaque, smiling, they continue to walk further into the dungeon.

Walking further, they find a pickax left by a corpse, the boy grabs it.
"They're giving us pieces to the puzzle that is breaking the wall?" asks Izaque, doubtful.
"I think so, let's go back to the wall to try to break it, bro," says Ana.
Then the siblings walk back to the wall, Izaque and Ana switch between hitting the wall with the pickax.
After some time, they manage to break the wall, pass through the hole and start to walk.
They discover that it is a hall that leads to a room, and on the way, they find a lot of pictures and old portraits.
"Do you think there's someone still alive here?" asks the boy, doubtful.
[1]

Suddenly, the boy notices a picture on his right and says, "Look! This is my grandpa".
The girl notices another picture and says, "There's a picture of my aunt here... There really is a stalker here".
"A stalker that knows my family and yours, why is he interested in them?" asks the boy in doubt.
"I don't know big bro, what your family has of importance?" asks the girl.
"Hmm, I don't know, they are a little rich, and... Maybe they have something important, a lot of story behind it," explains the boy.
"Maybe this guy knows something that we don't..." says the girl.

They walk a little more and find a library with a lot of books, the boy grabs one, opens it, and starts to read.
"Tim Burton, Day 23 of February of 1567, went fishing, came home and went to slept..." the boy interrupts his reading to do a comment, "This looks like a journal or a book from someone who was spying on another person," says the boy, gaping.
He starts to read a lot of books until he finds one of his grandfather.
"He has a boy by his side, seems to be his grandson, is looking at the waves of the sea..." the boy interrupts his reading again, "I remember exactly that day, this guy is really a stalker," says the boy, more surprised than ever.
"Okay, I think we should go discover who is this stalker... Do you think we will be in danger, big bro?" asks the girl, hugging her brother's arm, scared.
"No, I think we'll be fine, as long as we keep ourselves out of the sight of this stalker," replies Izaque, caressing her hair.

Suddenly, they hear a rumble and a stamp of feet.
"So brats are entering into my den eh?" asks a rough voice with a terrifying tone that appears from behind the duo, it is a man.
He puts his two hands on the necks of the duo and lifts them, the duo trembles from fear and the girl punches his face.
"I'll protect you, bro!" says Ana.
Ana takes a chair on the side and throws it at the man, it breaks, and he screams in pain and gets distracted.
Izaque grabs a bread roll that was on the table, "Me too, little sis!" says him, then he hits the head of the man a lot of times.

They run to hide, the boy brings the bread roll and the girl brings an iron shell, they hide in a place of the dungeon.
"Where are you brats!" says the angry man while throwing everything in the air to find them.
"Oh, the things we get ourselves into, bro," says Ana trying to keep her voice low so as not to be discovered, she giggles a little.
"Shush, little sis," says Izaque.
The girl grabs a stone and throws it on the opposite side where they are to call the attention of the man.
"Hmm?" says the man while walking in the direction of the noise of the stone.
"Where are you, I know you're there, show up!" says the man enraged while he searches for the two in the place where the stone is.
Ana signals to the boy, telling him to go that way, so they can escape the man.

They see that they are already far from the man and continue to walk.
"I think we lost him", says Ana, relieved, hugging Izaque to not get away from him.
"Yes, I'm glad you're safe, little sis," says Izaque.
"Me too, bro, hehe," says Ana smiling, who kisses Izaque's cheek.

After some time, they get used to the light and can see the room, they see a golden shiny sword stuck in a rock.
And also a stone sign is attached to the wall above the sword that says: "Only the worthy of this sword can wield it, only the person of the same blood as the creator is worthy enough to do so."
"Well, here I go," says Izaque, having faith in his strength and knowing that he is of the same blood as his grandfather.
Izaque walks forward and tries to lift the sword, after using a lot of strength, he manages to get the sword off the rock, he realizes that the scabbard is on the side.
He lifts the sword and it shines with the light of the candles around, looking like a real knight, he looks up, admiring the golden sword.
"Wow..." says Ana, surprised, looking mindfully at the blade of the sword, "You look like a real knight, big bro!" says her, smiling, admiring her brother.
"Thanks, little sis, hehe," says Izaque with his eyes shining, happy with his new weapon.

After verifying and looking around the room, both notices that this was the room of the grandfather of Izaque and this was his sword.
"Do you think he lived here? My grandpa, what he did here?" asks Izaque.
Then Ana replies, "I don't know, maybe studied a little, read books, and wrote too..."
"Is it just me or the stair is shining, big bro?" asks Ana.
"Looks like it is, I think it's for us to go up then," replies Izaque while giggling a little, he grabs the scabbard and wears it.
The glow of the stair gets intenser and the two start to walk on it, after some time walking, they arrive at an exit, there is some cool wind going on.
"Well, looks like that guy won't go after us anymore, little sis," says Izaque.
"He'll only go if he misses that... A shining sword..." says Ana, while looking at it.
"The sword has probably been there in that rock for many years, because he is not worthy to take it, so it's of no use to him," says Izaque.

The boy looks closely at the blade of the sword and sees the reflection of his grandfather in it, he looks back and sees no one.
He feels a connection to his grandfather and the sword as if it were destined to be his.
"Gee, you have a sword, and I don't even have a weapon, big bro..." says Ana a little sad.
"Hmm, I'm sure you'll find something for you, little sis, you deserve it," says Izaque, smiling and hugging his sister.
"Thanks, big bro, hehe," says Ana, returning the hug and beginning to smile again.
"I think we should go home and clean this dusty sword of yours, big bro," says her, who kisses Izaque's cheek.
"Okay, hehe," replies Izaque, smiling.
The two siblings start to go back home on foot, Izaque stores the sword on the scabbard that is on his waist.
