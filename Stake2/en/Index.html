Digital identity: Funkysnipa Cat









"Funkysnipa Cat" is an identity and account that I created in the social network Google+ around 2013.
Google+ is a social network created by Google in 2011, every account of YouTube at the time were Google+ accounts.
I created the Funkysnipa Cat account because of the Brazilian movement called "Funky Cats Movement".
The movement of the Funky Cats was a movement that happened on Google+ between 2013 and 2019, 2019 was when Google+ was closed by Google.
It consisted in honoring the Brazilian YouTuber Funky Black Cat, creating themes of other Funky Cats.
And replacing the "Black" of the name by the chosen theme by the person, editing the original picture of Funky Black Cat to match the theme.
Like Funky Pastry Cook Cat, Funky Ninja Cat, Funky Gamer Cat, Funky Musician Cat, Funky Jedi Cat.
My Funky Cat was the Funkysnipa Cat, because I wanted to be different and not have three words in the name.
The "Snipa" was because when Funky Black Cat was going to say "Sniper" he said "Snipa", and I also like the gun a lot.
I got to know a lot of things that I know to this day on Google+, with My Little Pony, Five Nights at Freddy's, Undertale, I started to like Dubstep more.
And also made a lot of friends, Google+ was closed in April 2, 2019 by Google because it had security and privacy issues that Google hid and people discovered.
People became sad because Google+ was closed, and migrated to MeWe, Google+ Rewritten, and other social networks.
But for me, I can not find a social network that surpasses or replaces Google+, better to keep the good times only in memory.
