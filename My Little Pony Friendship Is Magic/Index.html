My Little Pony: Friendship Is Magic (abbreviated as MLP: FIM) is an animated children's television series.
Based on the fourth incarnation of Hasbro's My Little Pony franchise.
The show follows a studious unicorn (later an alicorn) pony named Twilight Sparkle (Tara Strong) and her friends Applejack (Ashleigh Ball), Rarity (Tabitha St. Germain), Fluttershy (Andrea Libman), Rainbow Dash (Ball), Pinkie Pie (Libman), and dragon assistant Spike (Cathy Weseluck).
They travel on adventures and help others around Equestria while solving problems that arise in their own friendships.

Thank you for everything, I thank for this amazing Fandom, and for this amazing cartoon, for being part of my life, my childhood.
